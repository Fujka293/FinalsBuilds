Tips & Guides

Beginner

  • Understand class roles and their strengths.
  • Start with balanced builds to learn game flow.
  • Experiment with gadgets in the training area.

Intermediate

  • Adapt your build to the map and team composition.
  • Master movement and positioning for your class.
  • Utilize synergy between gadgets and weapons.

Advanced

  • Optimize loadouts for specific strategies (e.g., flanking, support).
  • Coordinate builds for maximum team efficiency.
  • Analyze enemy builds and counter-pick effectively.
