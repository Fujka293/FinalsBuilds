About FinalsBuilds

FinalsBuilds is a fan-made project designed to help players of The Finals optimize their gameplay through interactive tools, guides, and community-driven insights.

The site is not affiliated with Embark Studios or Nexon. All trademarks and images are property of their respective owners.

Inspirations & Sources

FinalsBuilds draws inspiration from a variety of sources to create a modern, user-friendly, and visually appealing experience for players of The Finals.

  • Stefano DeRosa's Portfolio: Modern design principles, clean layouts, and interactive UI elements.
  • Gaming Websites: Sites like Fall Guys, Apex Legends, and Valorant for dynamic animations and responsive design.
  • Community Wikis and Official Game Resources: For accurate and up-to-date information on weapons, gadgets, and gameplay mechanics.
