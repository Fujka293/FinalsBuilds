Beginner Tips

  • Test different loadouts and adapt to your team’s needs.
    Versatility is key for effective play.
  • Master movement: jump, climb, use zip lines, and reposition unpredictably.
    Mobility is as important as aim.
  • Learn each class’s strengths and movement options.
    Light: speed and stealth; Medium: balance; Heavy: durability and destruction.
  • Understand game modes: focus on objectives, not just kills.
    Cashout and Ranked require different strategies.

Intermediate Tips

  • Adapt builds to map and team composition.
    Some gadgets and weapons excel on certain maps.
  • Coordinate with your team for push/defend strategies.
    Communication and synergy win matches.
  • Use environmental destruction to your advantage.
    Break floors/walls to expose or trap enemies.
  • Synergize gadgets and specializations.
    Combine mesh shields with heavy weapons, or use smoke for stealth revives.

Advanced Tips

  • Counter-pick based on enemy builds.
    Choose gadgets and weapons that exploit enemy weaknesses.
  • Optimize builds for specific roles: flanker, support, anchor, disruptor, objective specialist.
  • Use advanced movement (double jumps, grappling) for creative plays.
  • Master cashout timing and baiting strategies.
    Force enemies into traps or split their focus.

Teamplay & Communication

  • Ping objectives and threats.
    Keep your team informed at all times.
  • Assign roles and builds before each round.
    Balance aggression, support, and defense.
  • Share gadgets and cover each other’s weaknesses.
    Team synergy is more important than individual skill.
